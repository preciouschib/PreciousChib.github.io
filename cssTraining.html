Welcome!

I am trying to obtain my bachelors degree in Studio Arts focusing with emphasis on Graphic Design. While doing so build my brand as a creator through social media. Interning within my field of study is also something I hope to accomplish within the next two years. These short term goals are very important because they influence my long term goals. They are the beginning steps. After I get my degree, depending on my financial state and job placement, I might either go straight into the workforce or try to obtain my Masters from Otis College of Art and Design or a UC that has a great graduate program. My ultimate goals are to improve on my skill set through education and hands on experience, and to grow personally with a career as a designer in hopes of one day becoming a prominent creative director and multimedia designer. After achieving my goal as a creator/designer, I hope to have enough money and experience life outside of California. I want to work with unprivileged young children and empower upcoming youth.

I can confidently say that I am a well rounded individual who sees value in all things creative and has passion for the arts. My passion for the arts has always been a part of my existence. It is common for career interests to change for many students as they get older, but for me it has always been art and design. Since I was little, I’ve been intrigued by my vibrant colors and quirky patterns. I discovered that design is my way of expressing my ideas, which ultimately leads to me turning those ideas into something real and original. I took on graphic design because It embodies my interest in design, precision, attention to detail and visually stimulating artwork that can tell a story. eauty in all things is possible.
